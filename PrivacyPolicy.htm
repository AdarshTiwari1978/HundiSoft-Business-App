1. Information We Collect

HundiSoft - Business Management is designed with privacy in mind. We collect and store the following information locally on your device:

1.1 Business Data

  • Customer information (names, addresses, phone numbers)
  • Transaction details (amounts, dates, interest rates)
  • Financial records and ledger entries
  • Business settings and preferences

1.2 Device Information

  • Device identifier for license validation
  • App usage statistics for functionality
  • Error logs for app improvement

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Local Data Storage

All your business data is stored locally on your device using an SQLite database. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device.
  • No internet connection is required for core functions.
  • You have complete control over your data.

2.2 License Validation

We use device identifiers solely for:

  • Validating your app license.
  • Preventing unauthorized use.
  • Ensuring proper app functionality.

2.3 App Functionality

We use your data to:

  • Provide business management features.
  • Generate reports and analytics.
  • Calculate financial transactions.
  • Export data to PDF format.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

3.1 No Data Transmission

We do not transmit your business data to any external servers or third parties. All data remains on your device.

3.2 No Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your business information with any third parties.

3.3 Export Functionality

When you export data to PDF, the files are created locally on your device and can be shared by you through your preferred method.

4. Data Security

4.1 Local Storage

Your data is stored securely in a local SQLite database on your device.

4.2 No Cloud Storage

We do not use cloud storage or external databases for your business data.

4.3 Device Security

Your data security depends on your device's security measures. We recommend:

  • Using device passwords/PINs.
  • Keeping your device updated.
  • Using secure backup methods.

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Data Access

You have complete access to all your data stored in the app.

5.2 Data Export

You can export your data to PDF format at any time.

5.3 Data Deletion

You can delete your data by:

  • Uninstalling the app.
  • Clearing app data through device settings.
  • Using the app's built-in data management features.

5.4 License Management

You can manage your app license through the app's license management features.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is designed for business use and is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • - Posting the new privacy policy in the app.
  • - Updating the policy on our website.
  • - Sending you an email notification.
