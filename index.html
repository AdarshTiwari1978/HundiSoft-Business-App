HundiSoft Business App

An easy app for hundi brokers to help them for hundi transactions management.
Generation of various reports like transaction report, ledger, bill (panna), due list and customer details etc.
Backup and recovery facility.
Works on any android device.
15 days trial period to test all functions.
Desktop version is also available.
